Welcome to the SimpleAI execution web viewer

Started running. On the top bar you have links to control the execution of the algorithm, and links to switch between the graph view, the log view, the stats view, and this help view. You can also press following keys to control execution: [e] Play, [enter] Step, [p] Pause and [q] Quit.

On the bottom bar you have information about the current step (hover with your mouse over it to see more details).

You can also zoom and pan on the graph view using your mouse wheel and buttons.

Happy debugging!
